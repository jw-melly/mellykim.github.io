An unusual taste or a style that might seem strange to others. It could stem from a deep passion and love for something—or, conversely, from a sense of profound lack or longing. In a way, everyone is somewhat fanatical about certain aspects of life. It’s a concept too ambiguous to define as purely good or bad.
To some, it might appear eccentric or even insane. But from where I stand, that very thing, grounded in reality yet shining brilliantly in my world, is more radiant than anything else.
