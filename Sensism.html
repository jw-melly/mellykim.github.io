I want to shine like the moon.
Time never gives me enough to explain why.
Because I’ve never seen anything as beautiful,
And because I’ve watched that moon every night.
I’ve drawn countless thoughts from it,
And fled there alone so many times.
No one will ever tell me I shine like the moon.
I don’t expect them to.
But still,

I hope someone beyond that ocean
Mistakes me for the moon.

I want to shine like the moon.
Because, The moon can light up someone’s night.
Because, it is beautiful simply by existing,
And because it brightens my nights every evening.
The moon illuminates both the paths I think about
And the path I flee.
I’ve never thought anyone I met shone like the moon.
But, you know,

Someone beyond that ocean—
Are you the moon?
